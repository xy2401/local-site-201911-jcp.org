 JSR #371
Model-View-Controller (MVC 1.0) Specification
JSR Renewal Ballot
 Ballot duration: 2016-11-29   to: 2016-12-12


Special Vote Instructions:

Dear EC members,

We are reaching out to you to request your approval of the continuation of JSR 371 in a renewal ballot.

The main goal of JSR 371 is to provide a standardized action-based Model-View-Controller framework.

JSR 371 has successfully completed two Early Draft releases, and, while its current specification leads have been diverted to other tasks, the expert group has continued to be very active and has made considerable progress in addressing open issues. The expert group discussions can be viewed here: https://java.net/projects/mvc-spec/lists/jsr371-experts/archive

When Oracle recently announced plans to discontinue this JSR, we heard from many impassioned community members that they would like to see the work of this JSR continue.

Oracle therefore plans to seek a new specification lead to whom this JSR can be transferred. In the meantime, we request that the renewal ballot for this JSR be approved so that the JSR will not be terminated.


These are the final results of the JSR Renewal Ballot for JSR #371. The EC has approved this ballot.

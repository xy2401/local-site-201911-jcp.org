Minutes

Personnel changes

Patrick reported Jason Gartner has replaced Mark Thomas as IBM's primary rep, Rui Nogueira is no longer an alternate for SAP, and Tony Baker is no longer an alternate for Intel. As previously noted, Time Warner Cable resigned their seat (see the notes on the special election, below.)

EC statistics

Due to lack of time the EC stats presentation was not discussed, merely inserted into the record.

The EC went into private session for a discussion with Steve Harris and Jeet Kaul on the approach that Oracle intends to take to the JCP.

Process improvements (jcp.next proposal)

The EC continued in private session for a discussion of proposals made by Patrick for a process improvement (jcp.next) JSR.

Special election

2007 activities summary (presentation)

Jyri Lassi (jyri.lassi@nokia.com) replaces Dietmar Tallroth as Nokia's alternate on the Java ME EC.

Schedules and locations for 2008 face to face meetings

The EC agreed on the schedules and locations for the next year's face-to-face meetings:

  • May 20-21 2008 in Paris, France (hosted by Orange France)
  • September 25-26 2008 on the US East Coast (host and exact location TBD)
  • January 13-14 2009 on the US West Coast host and exact (location TBD)

The full schedule is avalable on the calendar page at jcp.org..

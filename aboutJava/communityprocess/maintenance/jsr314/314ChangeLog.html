Change Log for JSR-000314 JavaServerTM Faces 2

This page details the proposed, accepted and deferred changes to JSR 314, documenting the changes that will go into the next revision, per Section 4.2 of the JCP 2.7 document. It contains a snapshot of the issue log for JSR 314, located here: http://wiki.jcp.org/wiki/index.php?page=JSF+2.0+Rev+A+Change+Log.

Last updated: 15 November 2010

ACCEPTED CHANGES
